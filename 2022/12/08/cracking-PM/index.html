Chapter 10. Company Research

  1. Do company research can impress your interviewer.

The Product

  1. Products
    What is the array of products or features that the company creates? How do the products fit each other?

  2. Competitors
    Who are the competitors? How does the company differentiate itself from them?

Ebay: Second hand items.

Provide a recommended price range for the items to sell. Minimum and maximum and average
ML model to predict best price for the item.

  1. Customers/Market
    What is the target market for the company? Are there any secondary market right now or that you would suggest the company enter?

  2. Revenue
    How does the company make money? How would you suggest the company make more money? What revenue strategies?

  3. Love and Hate
    How do customers feel about the product?

  4. Metrics
    Company’s key metrics

  5. News and rumors

The Strategy

  1. Mission
    How does the company live up to the mission? Be specific.

Our mission is to reimagine commerce in ways that build a more fulfilling and lasting world. We are building a human, authentic and community-centric global and local marketplace.

  1. Strategy
    What do you think is the company’s strategy? Are there any missteps with respect to that?
