novelty detection

Detect new instances that look different form all instances in training set.
Require very clean data.

If there are 99% of dogs and 1% are Chihuahua.
Novelty detection -> NOT treate Chihuahua as novelties.
Anomaly detection -> Treate Chihuahua as anomalies.
