2266. Count Number of Texts

  • 思路

    • For every number other than 7 and 9, we can consider at most 3 consecutive digits whereas for 7 and 9 we can consider atmost 4 consecutive digits.

  • Operation

    • Make an empty dp array.
    • Iterate through every key pressed in pressedKeys and for every i we can add dp[i-1],dp[i-2],dp[i-3] to dp[i] and if pressedKeys[i] is either 7 or 9 then we can add dp[i-4] also.
